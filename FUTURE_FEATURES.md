# Future Features

## Round Multiplier
Reward later rounds more to create comeback opportunities and keep the game exciting until the end. Later rounds would be worth more points (e.g. 1× early, 1.5× mid-game, 3× final rounds).

## Double Down
During Phase 2, players can optionally Double Down on their prediction before submitting. If correct (score ≥ 750) they earn 2× points. If wrong they earn 0. High risk, high reward mechanic.

## Player-Submitted Questions
Let players add their own custom questions before or during the game. Host approves/rejects before they enter the pool. Makes games more personal and replayable with different groups.
